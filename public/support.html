Welcome to the Support page for the License Check app.

Our goal is to provide you with the resources and support you need to get the most out of our app.

Github Issues:

If you encounter any bugs or issues with our License Check app, we encourage you to report them to our Github repository: https://github.com/fairdataihub/license-check. This repository is actively monitored, and we will do our best to respond to your issue in a timely manner.

Contact Us:

If you need to contact us directly, you can reach us by emailing help@fairdataihub. We are here to help and will respond to your request as soon as possible.

Thank you for using the License Check Probot app. We are constantly working to improve our app and make it the best it can be. If you have any suggestions or feedback, we would love to hear from you.
