Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/31/2023

Introduction:

At fairdataihub, we respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting the personal information that they entrust to us.
This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information that our Github app collects and how this information will be used.

Information Collection:

Our Github app collects information related to installation and push events. This information is necessary for the functioning of the app and to provide users with the services they expect.
The information collected is used to monitor and analyze usage patterns, troubleshoot issues, and improve the user experience.

Data Usage:

The information collected by our Github app will only be used to provide users with the services they expect.
We do not share this information with any third parties unless required by law.

Data Sharing:

We do not share any information collected by our Github app with third parties.

Data Retention:

The information collected by our Github app will only be stored for as long as it is necessary to provide users with the services they expect.
Once it is no longer needed, the information will be deleted or anonymized.

Data Security:

We take the security of the information collected by our Github app very seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure that the information is protected from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or destruction.

User Control:

Users have the right to access, rectify, or delete the information that is collected by our Github app.
If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.

Changes to Privacy Policy:

From time to time, we may need to update this Privacy Policy. If we do so, we will inform users by updating the Effective Date at the top of this page.
We encourage users to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed about how we are protecting their information.
